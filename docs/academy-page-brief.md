# Academy Page Brief

This document defines what goes on the /academy page and why. Reference this before making any changes to the page.

## The Job of This Page

Convert a self-conscious working professional — who is too embarrassed to take a public dance class — into a founding member. Every section must serve that one goal.

## Page Structure (Top to Bottom)

### 1. Hero
**Headline:** The transformation promise, not the product description.
- Lead with the outcome: confident, dancing in public, for real
- Image: group-class.jpg (social proof that real people are in this)
- Primary CTA: "Join as a Founding Member — $199"
- Secondary CTA: "See what's included ↓"

### 2. Who This Is For
Short, direct. Make the right person feel seen.
- Working professional
- Too self-conscious for a public class
- Wants to learn alone, at home, at their own pace
- Zero experience required

### 3. The Transformation
Before and after. Specific, not vague.
- Before: shy, no rhythm, avoids the dance floor
- After: walks into any party or club and dances — for real, not faking it

### 4. What You Get
Three concrete pillars:
- **Video curriculum** — structured, progressive, built by a 25-year instructor
- **Community** — private, judgment-free, everyone is a beginner
- **Live sessions with Ceech** — monthly group sessions, earn smaller group access over time

### 5. The Curriculum
The 6 modules, already on the page. Keep as-is.

### 6. The Tier System
Student → Practitioner → Veteran. Explain what each earns.
- Keeps students engaged long-term
- Makes the subscription feel like it grows with them

### 7. Pricing
Crystal clear. No surprises.
- Founding member: $199 first 3 months, then $29/month locked forever
- Regular: $297 first 3 months, then $37/month
- Founding member spots: progress bar (already on page)
- "Your $29/month rate is locked forever — no matter what we charge in the future"

### 8. The Funnel Context
One line: "Already a BeatFirst user? You've started Module 1."
Links BeatFirst users directly into the Academy narrative.

### 9. Waitlist Form / Buy CTA
The existing waitlist form stays until payment is set up.
Once Stripe/payment is integrated, replace with direct purchase.

## Tone
- Direct, not salesy
- Honest about what it is (still being built, founding member = early access)
- Warm but confident — like Ceech talks in person
- Never overpromise. The transformation is real but takes work.

## What NOT to Put on This Page
- Testimonials (don't have them yet — add in Phase 2 when founding members have results)
- Exact launch date (don't have one — "coming soon" kills urgency)
- Too much detail about the live sessions (specifics TBD)
