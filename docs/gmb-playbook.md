## 5. Review request templates

**Goal:** 56 → 75 GMB reviews by end of Q2 (19 new reviews). That's ~3/month if you systematically ask every private-lesson student.

### SMS template (send immediately after the lesson, while the good feeling is fresh)

```
Hi [NAME] — thanks for today's lesson! If you have 60 seconds, would you mind leaving a quick Google review? It genuinely helps other people find me. Here's the direct link:

[YOUR GMB REVIEW LINK — get it from GMB dashboard → Home → "Get more reviews" → Share]

No pressure at all if now's not a good time. — Ceech
```

### Email template (backup, for when SMS isn't a fit)

**Subject:** A quick favor — Google review?

```
Hey [NAME],

Thanks again for the session today. I really enjoyed [SPECIFIC DETAIL FROM THE LESSON — e.g., "how quickly you picked up the footwork on that Smurf sequence"].

If you have a minute, I'd really appreciate a short Google review. It makes a genuine difference in helping other students find me — word of mouth on Google is most of how new students discover me.

Direct link: [YOUR GMB REVIEW LINK]

Doesn't need to be long. Anything honest is great.

Appreciate it,
Ceech
```

### Pro tip
Send it the SAME DAY as the lesson. Conversion rates on review asks crater after 48 hours because the feeling fades. Put a reminder in your calendar: "Send review request to [last student]" at the end of each teaching day.
