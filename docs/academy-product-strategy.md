# DanceWithCeech Academy — Product & Go-To-Market Strategy

## The Transformation Promise

From shy and rhythm-deaf to confidently dancing in public — moving to the music in a way that looks and feels real. Not fake confidence. Earned confidence. Students finish this program knowing they look good because the skill is real, and other people can see it.

## Who This Is For

Working professionals who are self-aware and too embarrassed to take a public dance class. They want to learn privately, at home, on their own schedule, without being judged by classmates. Age doesn't matter. Zero skill is fine. The only requirement is that they want to learn.

**End goal for the student:** Walk into a wedding, corporate party, house party, or club — and dance. Enjoy themselves. Know they are moving to the rhythm of the music. Not faking it.

## The Customer Funnel

```
BeatFirst (free)
    → Academy ($199 founding / $297 regular — first 3 months)
        → Private Lessons ($80–$250/session)
```

Each product feeds the next. BeatFirst proves the teaching method works before anyone pays. The Academy builds the foundation. Private lessons are the premium refinement for students who want direct 1-on-1 coaching.

## Product Structure

### What's Included
- Full structured video curriculum (built once, scales infinitely)
- Private online community (judgment-free, nurturing environment)
- Monthly live group session with Ceech
- Progress tracking through modules
- Monthly challenges and accountability

### The Curriculum (6 Modules)
1. **Rhythm & Timing** — The foundation every dancer skips (preview available free via BeatFirst)
2. **Basic Grooves** — The bounce, the rock, the two-step
3. **Hip-Hop Fundamentals** — Core moves with real musical context
4. **Footwork & Floor Patterns** — Moving through space with intention
5. **Locking Foundations** — Funk discipline, locks, points, wrist rolls
6. **Freestyle & Musicality** — Respond to music in real time

### The Tier System

| Tier | Requirement | Benefits |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **Student** | Month 1+ | Full curriculum, community, monthly group live session |
| **Practitioner** | 3 consecutive months + 5 practice videos posted | Everything above + small group session with Ceech (8–12 people) |
| **Veteran** | 6 consecutive months + all modules completed | Everything above + earned 1-on-1 time with Ceech (details TBD) |

**Philosophy:** Time alone is not enough. Activity is required. Students who stay and do the work earn more access to Ceech. This rewards commitment and protects Ceech's time as the academy scales.

## Pricing

| | Founding Members (first 100) | Regular |
|---|---|---|
| **First 3 months** | $199 | $297 |
| **Monthly after month 3** | $29/month — locked forever | $37/month |

**Founding member benefits:**
- Discounted entry ($199 vs $297)
- Permanently locked at $29/month regardless of future price increases
- Recognition as a founding member in the community

**Why this works:**
- $199 upfront filters serious students only
- $29/month locked forever creates lifelong loyalty and word-of-mouth
- Regular price increase gives Ceech pricing flexibility as the community grows
- The progress bar (spots claimed / 100) creates urgency

**Financial projections (illustrative):**
- 100 founding members × $199 = $19,900 upfront
- 60% retained at $29/month = $1,740/month recurring
- At 500 students, 60% retained at $37/month = $11,100/month recurring

## The Dead Community Problem — Solution

The community will be seeded with current community college students — free first month in exchange for being active members. They already know and trust Ceech. They will post, engage, and make the community feel alive for incoming paying members.

Ceech posts daily for the first 30 days — drills, tips, challenges, feedback. The community is never dead if Ceech is active. Students must post at least one practice video per month to maintain active status.

## Passive Income Model

The curriculum is built once. The community self-sustains with the right structure. Ceech's only recurring time commitment is monthly live sessions — which scale by tier (large group → small group → 1-on-1). As the academy grows, the per-student time cost decreases while recurring revenue increases.

## Phase 2 — Growth Mechanic: BeatFirst Referral Unlock

### The Idea
BeatFirst currently has 6 beat patterns. Lock the last 3. Users unlock them by referring friends.

### The Rules
- 1 referral = 1 beat pattern unlocked
- 3 referrals = all 3 beats unlocked
- A referral counts when the referred user creates an account AND completes at least one full game (prevents fake email abuse)

### Why It Works
- Zero cost — rewards are content already built
- Viral by design — every locked user becomes a salesperson
- Reward fits the product — unlocking beats is on-brand
- Builds the email list — all referred users must sign up

### On Cheating
Some fake accounts are acceptable. A user determined enough to create fake emails is your most engaged user — and your most likely Academy customer. Simple fraud prevention (must complete one game) is sufficient. Do not over-engineer.
