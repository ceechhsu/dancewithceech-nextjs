## Introduction

The Puppet is a captivating and iconic dance move that belongs to the vibrant world of Funk Style dancing. This mesmerizing technique creates the illusion that the dancer is being controlled by invisible strings, much like a marionette. The Puppet move is characterized by sharp, angular movements combined with fluid transitions, giving the impression that an unseen puppeteer is manipulating the dancer's body. This dance move not only showcases a dancer's control and precision but also adds a theatrical element to performances, making it a favorite among both dancers and audiences. The Puppet has become a staple in funk and street dance routines, often used to add a touch of whimsy and technical skill to choreographies.

## Dance History

The Puppet move emerged in the 1970s as part of the broader Funk Style dance movement. While the exact creator is not definitively known, it is widely attributed to the pioneering dancers of the West Coast funk scene, particularly in Los Angeles. The move gained popularity alongside other funk styles like locking and popping, becoming a key element in the funk dance vocabulary. Its development coincided with the rise of funk music and the growing street dance culture of the era. The Puppet move was further popularized through dance shows, music videos, and street performances throughout the 1980s and 1990s, cementing its place in dance history.

## Step By Step Instruction

1. Start in a neutral standing position with your feet shoulder-width apart.
2. Imagine strings attached to your wrists, elbows, knees, and head.
3. Begin with your arms. Lift your right arm as if pulled by a string, then let it drop suddenly.
4. Repeat with your left arm, creating a jerky, puppet-like movement.
5. Move to your legs. Lift your right knee, then drop it quickly.
6. Alternate between arms and legs, maintaining sharp, sudden movements.
7. Incorporate your head by tilting it to one side, then letting it drop as if the string was cut.
8. Combine these movements, varying the rhythm and order for a more dynamic effect.
9. Add a slight bounce to your stance to enhance the puppet-like appearance.
10. Practice transitioning smoothly between movements while maintaining the illusion of being controlled.

## Tips For Learning

• Start slow and focus on isolating each body part individually
• Use a mirror to check your form and the overall puppet-like effect
• Practice to different tempos of music to improve your timing and rhythm
• Exaggerate the sharpness of your movements for a more dramatic effect
• Incorporate facial expressions to enhance the puppet character
• Study videos of skilled dancers performing the Puppet move for inspiration
• Combine the Puppet with other funk style moves for a more comprehensive routine

## Conclusion

The Puppet move is a testament to the creativity and expressiveness of Funk Style dancing. By mastering this technique, dancers can add a unique and visually striking element to their performances. The move's combination of sharp control and fluid transitions challenges dancers to improve their body awareness and precision. As you practice and perfect the Puppet, remember that the key lies in creating the illusion of being controlled while maintaining your own artistry and style. Embrace the funky spirit of this iconic move and let it pull your dancing to new heights.
