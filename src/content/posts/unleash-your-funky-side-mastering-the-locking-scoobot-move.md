## Introduction

The Scoobot is a dynamic and playful move that epitomizes the essence of locking dance. Originating from the creative minds of locking pioneers, this move combines elements of the classic robot with the smooth and funky style of locking. It's a crowd-pleaser that showcases the dancer's ability to transition seamlessly between robotic and fluid movements, adding a touch of humor and personality to any routine.

## Dance History

The Scoobot emerged in the early days of locking, a dance style that gained popularity in the 1970s. As locking pioneers like Don Campbell and The Lockers crew experimented with new moves, they drew inspiration from various sources, including the robot dance craze of the time. The Scoobot was born as a fusion of these influences, blending the mechanical movements of the robot with the smooth and groovy essence of locking.

## Step By Step Instruction

1. Start in a standing position with your feet shoulder-width apart and knees slightly bent.
2. Raise your arms in front of you, bending at the elbows to create a 90-degree angle.
3. Keeping your arms and body rigid, begin to move your feet in a shuffling motion, taking small steps forward or backward.
4. As you shuffle, rotate your torso and arms in a robotic manner, mimicking the movements of a robot.
5. Transition smoothly between robotic and fluid locking movements, incorporating elements like wrist rolls, points, and locks.
6. Add personality by exaggerating facial expressions, head movements, and incorporating playful gestures.

## Tips For Learning

- Practice the robotic movements first, focusing on keeping your body rigid and precise.
- Incorporate locking elements gradually, starting with simple wrist rolls or points.
- Experiment with different tempos and rhythms to add variety to your Scoobot.
- Study videos of locking pioneers and their unique interpretations of the Scoobot.
- Have fun with it and let your personality shine through the move.

## Conclusion

The Scoobot is a quintessential locking move that showcases the versatility and creativity of this dynamic dance style. By mastering the blend of robotic and fluid movements, you can add a touch of humor and funk to your routines, captivating audiences with your ability to seamlessly transition between contrasting styles. Embrace the playful spirit of the Scoobot and let your personality shine through this iconic locking move.
