## Introduction

The Prep is a classic hip hop dance move that originated in the 90s era but is still widely used in contemporary hip hop choreography. This move is all about mimicking the actions of getting ready or "prepping" to go out. It's a fun and expressive dance that allows you to showcase your personality and groove. With its roots in the golden age of hip hop, the Prep is a must-learn for any hip hop dancer.

## Dance History of The Prep

The Prep dance move emerged from the vibrant hip hop scene of the 1990s. While its exact origins are unclear, it is believed to have been popularized by dancers and choreographers in major cities like Los Angeles and New York. The move's name and actions are inspired by the routine preparations people go through before leaving their homes, such as checking their appearance in the mirror or brushing off lint from their clothes.

## Step By Step Instruction

1. Start with a bounce or groove, bending your knees and keeping your shoulders, arms, and hands loose.

2. Check the mirror: Bring your hands up to your face as if checking your appearance in a mirror, bouncing twice.

3. Brush off: Mimic brushing off lint or dandruff from your shoulders, brushing twice.

4. Clean off: Bend down and brush off any specks or dirt from your jeans, bouncing twice.

5. Bop: Stand up and do a final bop or bounce to complete the move.

## Tips For Learning The Prep

- Keep a consistent groove or bounce throughout the move.

- Exaggerate the actions to make them more expressive and entertaining.

- Add your own personal flair or style to the move.

- Practice the move slowly at first, then gradually increase the speed.

- Have fun with it and don't be afraid to show your personality!

## Conclusion

The Prep is a classic hip hop dance move that has stood the test of time. Its simple yet expressive actions make it a great addition to any hip hop routine or freestyle session. By mastering the Prep, you'll not only pay homage to the golden era of hip hop but also showcase your groove and personality on the dance floor.
