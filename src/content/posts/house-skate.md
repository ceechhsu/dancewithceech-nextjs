## Unlocking the Hip Hop House 'Skate' Move

Immerse yourself in the flow of the Hip Hop House "Skate," a move that captures the essence of gliding across the dance floor with effortless finesse. This dynamic step takes inspiration from the fluid motion of roller skating, allowing dancers to whisk through their space with a combination of slick footwork and smooth body rolls. Whether on the crowded floors of a bustling club or the spotlight of a dance battle, the Skate is a pivotal move for those looking to amp up their performance with slick, continuous motion and the suave essence of Hip Hop House.

## Dance History of The Skate

Hip Hop and House dance have always been innovators in borrowing and transforming elements from diverse activities into dance. The Skate move in Hip Hop House follows this tradition, pulling from the playful and fluid moves one might see on a roller rink. Despite not being associated with a single creator, the Skate surfaced as dancers emulated the rolling movement of skaters, embodying the spirit of freedom and flow. It's a representation of urban dance cultures' capacity to evolve and embrace the moves that resonate with the music and energy of the time.

## Step By Step Instruction

1. Begin with your feet shoulder-width apart, knees slightly bent.

2. Shift your weight onto your right foot, preparing to push off.

3. Glide your left foot forward and to the side, emulating the motion like you're gliding on skates.

4. As you draw your left foot back in, shift your weight to the left foot and repeat the motion on the opposite side.

5. Use your arms to balance and enhance the gliding sensation by swinging them gently in opposition to your legs.

6. Continue to alternate legs, maintaining a rhythmic and fluid motion, building momentum as you progress.

## Tips For Learning The Skate

- Focus on the fluidity of your movement; it should feel like you're actually skating.

- Gradually build up the speed, maintaining control over your leg glides.

- Incorporate body rolls or upper body movement to add style to your Skate.

- Practice on a smooth surface to help mimic the sensation of gliding.

- Keep your center of gravity low to maintain balance and smooth transitions.

## Conclusion

The Skate is a signature move in Hip Hop House that symbolizes the fusion of controlled footwork and uninhibited flow. It is a crowd-pleaser and a seamless addition to any dancer's repertoire, offering a breezy transition or a central routine focus. Whether you're burning up the dance floor solo or syncing with a crew, the smooth, rhythmic essence of the Skate solidifies your place in the pulsating heart of Hip Hop House.

- [How to Do the Crosswalk House Dance Move](/blog/house-crosswalk) — the Crosswalk is another traveling move with a gliding character, but it adds cross-steps that give the footwork more visual complexity than the Skate's clean diagonal push.
- [House Dance | Roger Rabbit](/blog/house-roger-rabbit) — both the Skate and the Roger Rabbit involve a trailing foot that sweeps loosely behind the lead foot, so the drag-and-glide mechanic is closely related between the two moves.
- [House Dance | Train](/blog/house-dance-move-train) — the Train is a foundational side-to-side traveling move that develops the same spatial awareness and rhythmic momentum that makes the Skate feel controlled rather than stumbling.
