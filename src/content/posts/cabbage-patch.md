## Dance History of The Cabbage Patch Dance

The Cabbage Patch emerged during the golden age of hip hop in the 1980s, named after the then-popular Cabbage Patch Kids dolls. Although the creator of this iconic move is not definitively known, it quickly became a staple in pop culture, often breaking out during celebrations and prominently featuring in music videos and dance routines. The universal appeal of the Cabbage Patch lies in its simplicity and the joyous sentiment it evokes.

## Step By Step Instruction

1. Begin with your feet shoulder-width apart, maintaining a slight bend in your knees.

2. Extend your arms out to the side of your body, bent at the elbows, with hands closed in fists.

3. Swing your arms in outward circular motions. Imagine drawing big circles in the air with your fists.

4. While your arms are circling, start swaying your hips from side to side in rhythm with the music.

5. Keep your movements relaxed and let your whole body get into the groove.

## Tips For Learning The Cabbage Patch

- The key to nailing the Cabbage Patch is to relax and enjoy the process.

- Focus on feeling the rhythm and let your movements be guided by the beat.

- Start off slow, mastering the arm circles before adding in the hip sway.

- Once comfortable, speed up and add energy to your movements.

- Remember, personality makes this dance pop, so feel free to add your own style!
